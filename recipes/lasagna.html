Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an Italian dish consisting of lasagne baked with meat or vegetables and a cheese sauce.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
